School Management Services

Services we provide

Orbital Node provides you with modern cutting edge school management system technology which shall make your school an icon in the society.

School Website

Orbital Node provides a fully functional website for all registered schools where news and information about the given schools shall be shared with the public. Also, parents shall communicate with the school authoririties about the welfare of their kids.

Students Portal

We also provide a student portal integrated in the school website where every information about a student such as class attendance and behavioural activities shall be accessed, school fees shall be paid and students results shall also be checked.

Results Computation

We provide an Automatic Results Computation System where the School shall input the student's scores and our system shall Automatically compute the Results. Configurations of the individual school grading systems shall be made via the school's Orbital Node Admin Panel. The Results shall be Prepared in a Printable format and placed in each Student's Portal in the School Website.

Orbital Node Features

School Website

This shall contain all the information about the school; a brief history of the school, pictorial review, services available, location of the school and the contact information.

Student Follow Up Platform

This section shall make provision for the school to update the students' everyday development, capturing the cognitive, affective and the psychomotor domains;the students' attendance to class.

Fees Payment Platform

The tuition fees and all other payments made to the school shall be performed through the app. This section shall help keep track of the transactions done in the school for easy accounting.

Students Attendance E-Register

The class attendance register shall be customized in an app that teachers shall use to mark the student's everyday attendance to classes same way as in the manual register.

Automatic Results Computation

The entire processes of results computation shall be carried out using an app that shall provide offline services to compute and manage both terminal and annual student's results that shall hereafter, be updated on the server to appear on the school's portal and in the student's follow-up platform.

Secured Database Information

All aspects incorporated into the portal shall be usable and accessible only by the school. The school shall set a login code to enable her manage the security of her portal. The very work supplied to the school in form of a mobile application cannot be transferred to another school for use.
